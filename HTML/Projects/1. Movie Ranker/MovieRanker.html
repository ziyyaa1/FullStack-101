My Bollywood Fav's

1. Suspense / Thriller / Mystery

Talaash

A broody, haunting mystery with layers of emotion and grief. Aamir Khan plays a cop with pain behind the badge. Must-watch for that slow-burn mystery thrill.

Drishyam

A mind-blowing thriller that keeps you hooked till the very end. Vijay Salgaonkar is THAT guy. Twists, suspense, emotion – it’s all there. Absolute must-watch, both parts!

Chor Nikal k Bhaga

High-stakes hijack + heist combo! Yami and Sunny deliver suspense, sass, and surprises. Just when you think you’ve figured it out — boom, another twist.

Dhoka

A lesser-known gem with major “trust no one” energy. It’s messy, dramatic, and keeps you second-guessing everyone. Chaos, betrayal, and a dark twist that makes you go “WAIT WHAT?

2. Horror

Horror Story

A group of friends. An abandoned hotel. One wild night. Classic horror setup but with some solid creepy moments. Ghosts, screams, and a surprisingly good pace — a spooky little thrill ride!

13B

A haunted TV that shows your future? Yup, that's 13B. Underrated but unforgettable horror!

3. Romance

Veer-Zaara

As an all-time SRK fan, this one is one of my fav masterpiece of him. Pure, poetic, timeless love. Cross-border romance with soul and sacrifice. An emotional masterpiece that lives rent-free in my heart.

Shiddat

ntense, passionate, and sometimes chaotic — just like young love. Sunny Kaushal gives *major loverboy energy*. It’s dramatic, dreamy, and definitely a cry-a-little movie.

Laila Majnu

Modern take on a legendary love story. Wild, poetic, and tragic AF. Imtiaz Ali's vibes, soulful music, and raw emotion — this one hits different. True hopeless romantic energy.

Okay Jaanu

Live-in, careers, and modern love dilemmas — but make it cute. Aditya + Shraddha’s chemistry is adorable, and AR Rahman’s music? Chef’s kiss. Light, sweet, and totally rewatchable.
